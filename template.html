The ICSC-CITIES committee invites you to contribute a manuscript for publication in the V Ibero-American Conference on Smart Cities (ICSC-CITIES 2022). ICSC-CITIES 2022 will take place on November 28-30, 2022, in Cuenca, Ecuador, with the sponsorship of the Universidad de Cuenca, Ecuador. All accepted articles will be published in the proceedings book of the congress. The Program Committee will select the best-accepted articles (written in English) for submission to Springer Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series (citescore 0.8 Scopus; SJR 0.16 - Q4 Computer Science). In addition, authors of selected articles will be invited to extend their contribution to be submitted for publication in Special Issues of the following journals:
  • Sensors (open access journal published by MDPI, citescore 5.8 Scopus–Q1; SJR 0.8–Q1 Analytical Chemistry, Q1 Instrumentation; JCR 3.576–Q1 Instruments & Instrumentation),
  • Sustainability (open access journal published by MDPI, SJR 0.612–Q1 Geography, Planning and Development, Q2 Renewable Energy, Sustainability and the Environment, Q2 Energy Engineering and Power Technology; JCR 3.251–Q2 Environmental Sciences),
  • Applied Sciences (open access journal published by MDPI, citescore 3.0 Scopus–Q2; SJR 0.44–Q2 Computer Science Applications, Engineering, Instrumentation; JCR 2.679–Q2 engineering, physics),
  • Revista Facultad de Ingeniería Universidad de Antioquia (citescore 0.8 Scopus–Q2; SJR 0.16–Q4; JCI 0.17–Q4 Engineering),
  • SN Computer Science (recently indexed in Scopus), published by Springer Nature.
  • Clean Energy and Sustainability published by SCIEPublish.
